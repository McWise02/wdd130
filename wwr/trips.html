Our Fantastic Trips

Experience the heart of Germany with a scenic yet thrilling rafting adventure down the Main River! As you float through charming towns and past historic landmarks, you'll be immersed in the rich culture and stunning landscapes of the Franconian countryside. The river offers a perfect mix of relaxing stretches and exciting rapids, so you can enjoy a fun, family-friendly outing or a more challenging thrill ride, depending on your level. The beauty of vineyards, the sight of castles, and the peaceful yet exciting flow of the water combine to make this rafting trip a must-do experience. Plus, you’ll have the chance to spot wildlife along the riverbanks and take in the breathtaking views from a unique perspective.

Get ready for an adrenaline-packed adventure down the iconic Rhein River! As you paddle through crystal-clear waters, you’ll be surrounded by majestic castles perched on cliffs and lush vineyards stretching as far as the eye can see. The thrills come with the challenge of navigating exciting rapids and swift currents, making it an unforgettable journey for both beginners and seasoned rafters. Along the way, discover the beauty of the Swiss and German landscapes, from charming villages to towering forests, all while sharing laughs with your crew. This is the perfect blend of natural beauty, history, and action-packed fun!

Ready to experience Vienna from a whole new angle? Paddle down the picturesque rivers that wind through this historic city, where urban sophistication meets nature's wild side! As you navigate gentle rapids and calm stretches of water, you’ll pass famous landmarks, such as the majestic St. Stephen's Cathedral and the stunning architecture lining the riverbanks. The tranquil atmosphere, combined with the occasional splash of excitement, makes this trip the ideal way to blend the beauty of the city with the fun of rafting. Whether you’re a rafting newbie or an experienced adventurer, Vienna’s river rafting adventure offers something unforgettable for everyone!
